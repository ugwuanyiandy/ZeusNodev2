How to compound a node
Step 1: Click on the “Deposit” Button.
Step 2: Select Node Tier Rewards.
Step 3: Click the “Fund” Button.
Step 4: Approve transaction.
Step 5: Wait till “Compound Balance” shows “New Balance”
Step 6: Enter node name at node tier.
Step 7: Click the “Compound Node” Button.
Step 8: Approve Transaction.
Tax System
-Less than 20 days - 80% claim tax
-More than 20 days - 15% claim tax
-Claim All button - 80% tax
-Compound, Buy/Sell- 0% tax
-Claim All button will always apply 80% tax, Use “Claim Selected” for 15% tax when “15% Active” status shows
